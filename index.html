About Our Lodge

Brotherhood, Charity & Truth Since 1892

Who We Are

Lights of the East Lodge No. 33 is a fraternal organization dedicated to the principles of brotherly love, relief, and truth. We welcome men of good character who seek to improve themselves and serve their community.

Our lodge has been a cornerstone of this community for over a century, bringing together men from all walks of life under the shared values of integrity, charity, and fellowship.

History

A Legacy of Brotherhood Spanning Generations

Our Founding

Chartered in 1892, our lodge was established by a small group of dedicated Freemasons who sought to bring the light of Masonry to this region. Through decades of growth, we have remained steadfast in our mission.

From humble beginnings to a thriving brotherhood, our history reflects the enduring strength of the Masonic principles that have guided us through generations of service, fellowship, and community leadership.

Scholarships

Investing in the Future of Our Community

Annual Scholarship

Each year we award scholarships to deserving students in our community. Applicants are evaluated on academic achievement, character, and community involvement.

Eligibility

Open to graduating high school seniors and current college students with demonstrated financial need and a record of community service. Deadline and award amounts to be announced.

How to Apply

Contact the lodge secretary for application materials. Completed applications must be submitted before the posted deadline along with two letters of recommendation.

In Memoriam

Honoring Brothers Who Have Passed to the Celestial Lodge Above

Remembering Our Brothers

We pause to honor the memory of our departed brothers who walked beside us in the light of Masonry. Their lives exemplified the values of our craft — integrity, compassion, and service.

Though they have passed to the Celestial Lodge Above, their legacy endures in the hearts of every brother they touched. Their names will be read aloud at each stated meeting in eternal remembrance.
